This website is three things at once: a record of my research in nanoscience, a home for the
scientific software I build — especially **NanoSami** — and a public notebook where I write about
what I learn along the way.

I believe research is better when its tools and methods are open and reproducible. That principle
drives both my lab work on ZnO nanoparticles and the software I write to analyze the data they
produce.
